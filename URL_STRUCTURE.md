# WorldCareers Rwanda - SEO URL Structure

## Main Pages
- Homepage: `https://worldcareers.rw/`
- All Jobs: `https://worldcareers.rw/jobs`
- Blogs: `https://worldcareers.rw/blogs`
- Career Insights: `https://worldcareers.rw/career-insights`
- About: `https://worldcareers.rw/about`

## Job Detail Pages
Pattern: `https://worldcareers.rw/job/{job-id}`

Example: `https://worldcareers.rw/job/12345`

## Tips for Content Creation

### District Pages
Each district page should target:
- "[District] jobs"
- "jobs in [District]"
- "[District] employment"
- "work in [District]"

### Category Pages
Each category page should target:
- "[Category] jobs Rwanda"
- "[Job Title] Rwanda"
- "[Industry] careers Kigali"
- "Rwanda [Category] vacancies"

### Blog Content Ideas
- "Top 10 Tech Companies Hiring in Kigali"
- "How to Write a CV for Rwanda Job Market"
- "Salary Guide: Finance Jobs in Rwanda 2025"
- "Remote Work Opportunities in Rwanda"
- "Interview Tips for Rwanda Employers"

### Career Insight Topics
- Rwanda Employment Statistics
- Industry Growth Trends
- Skills in Demand
- Employer Spotlights
- Professional Development Resources
